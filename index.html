About Me

I'm a marketing operations leader who thrives at the center of complexity. I specialize in owning campaigns end to end, connecting creative vision to operational execution, and have had the privilege of working at the most recognized entertainment and tech brands in the world. I began managing individual pieces of campaigns at Blizzard Entertainment and HBO Max, took on international campaign ownership at Apple, and now run multi-title global campaigns across brand, events, series, and film simultaneously at Netflix.

I operate with full visibility across the campaign: every stakeholder, every handoff, every risk. I am known for arriving prepared with several contingency plans and for the ability to pivot quickly, think clearly under pressure, and keep campaigns on track no matter what changes. I build trust quickly across every team to ensure the campaign ships on time, on budget, and set the standard for how great campaigns get made.
